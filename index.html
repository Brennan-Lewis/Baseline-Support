How can we help?

Baseline is built to stay out of your way. If something isn't working the way you expect, we want to know.

Common Questions

My purchases aren't showing up after I subscribed.

Open Baseline, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases." If that doesn't resolve it, try signing out of the App Store and back in, then restore again.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone and cancel from there. You'll retain Pro access through the end of your billing period.

I lost my workout data after updating the app.

Your data is stored locally on your device. If you've recently restored from a backup or switched devices, the data may not have transferred. Contact us and we'll do our best to help recover it.

Can I export my training data?

Yes — data export is included with Baseline Pro. You can access it from the Settings screen.

Something in the app isn't working right.

Send us a note with what you were doing and what happened. Screenshots help. We read every message.

Get in touch
